The problem

Property Verification in Bangladesh Is Complicated

Many property records are paper-based, scanned, handwritten, fragmented, or incomplete. This often creates ownership disputes, fraudulent document claims, missing transfer records, unpaid tax issues, and unclear inheritance history.

Fraudulent Ownership Claims

Fake or inconsistent ownership claims are difficult for ordinary buyers to identify.

Missing Legal Documents

Mutation records, khatian copies, tax receipts, or inheritance papers may be incomplete.

Complex Ownership History

Properties transferred across multiple generations can be hard to trace manually.

Manual Verification Delay

Traditional document checking is slow, costly, and prone to human error.
